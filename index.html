Room Escape Amsterdam

Room Escape Amsterdam, Play this exciting Game live in our brand new location in Amsterdam West

Room Escape Amsterdam is one of the best getaways for both individuals and businesses. The concept is very simple, you will be imprisoned, together with your friends, colleagues or relatives in one of our Room Escape rooms. A Room Escape Game lasts up to 1 hour but the sport is, of course, to escape from the room as quickly as possible. You do this by solving puzzles, clues and good cooperation.

Our Rooms

Room Escape Amsterdam is powered by Kunstgras Events and currently has four rooms in Amsterdam West. Each room has its own theme. We are often asked which room is best? This is very dependent on your group. Read the descriptions of the rooms for yourself, “Room 8“, ” Quarantine 2.0“, “The Bunker” and “Vietnamese Victim” . There you can instantly see the availability and possibly book a room.

Room Escape Amsterdam Arrival and Reception

We ask you at least 15 minutes before your Room Escape Amsterdam adventure to be present at our location. Would you like to sit down and enjoy a cup of coffee or tea, please make sure that you are at our place 30 minutes before. Make sure you check the travel directions on wich location you are expected.

Room Escape Amsterdam for Groups

Our location is suitable to accommodate individuals and big busines groups from 2 to 500 people. You can combine your Room Escape adventure with a meeting, drinks, BBQ, workshops or other teambuilding activities. For every group, big or small, we can make a quotation that suits your needs.

Room Escape at your location

Beside our own Room Escape in Amsterdam, and where you are more then welcome, there is now the ability to play an escape game at your location. We currently have 4 different game concepts, the Room Escape Challenge, Room Escape Tents, our Room Escape Bus and our City Escape Game. You can play these games with up to 2,000 participants. We will take you with us into the world of real life gaming. During the games, the teams will have to work together as time ticks away. Stress, ad hoc decisions and euphoria alternate. Think fast, mind games, solve puzzles and you will have to make hard choices.

Room Escape at our location

Room Escape Amsterdam is located at the Tom Schreursweg 8, you can park here for free. There is a direct bus from Amsterdam Central Station, here you will find the directions.
